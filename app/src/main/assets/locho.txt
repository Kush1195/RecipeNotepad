Here is The Receipi of Locho. Master Chef Tarla Dalal Explains the easiest way of cooking Khaman Dhokla ( Gujarati Recipe). Method : 1. Combine the besan, semolina, sugar, ginger-green chilli paste, citric acid and salt along with little water (approximately ¾ cup) in a bowl and mix well with the help of your hand to get a smooth and thick batter
