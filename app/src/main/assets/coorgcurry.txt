How to Make Coorg Curry (Pork Curry) : Take a pot, add 1/2 kg pork, water, salt and let it simmer for 40 minutes. In the meanwhile prepare the masala. For the masala:Take a pan, add fenugreek seeds, cumin seeds, coriander seeds and pepper. Mix well. Roast it till it changes color. Once roasted, powder the mix and use it as a masala. Keep aside. For the masala paste:Take a pan, add onions, ginger,cloves, curry leaves and small green chillies. Mix well. Let the onions turn brown in color. Once cooked, add a handful of coriander leaves and oil so that the ingredients don't stick to each other. Mix and remove from heat. Now grind this paste nicely. Keep aside.
