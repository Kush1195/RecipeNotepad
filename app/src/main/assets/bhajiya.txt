Bhajiya

Here is The Receipi of Bhajiya. Mix together first 4 dry ingredients.Dry roast cumin and ajwain together in a fry pan until the colour changes a few shades darker then crush using a rolling pin.Add to dry ingredients.Add cabbage, onion, garlic, chili, salt.Add water a little at time until dropping consistency.Mix well.Deep fry spoonfuls until golden brown.Drain on paper towel. Serve with raita or tamarind chutney.
