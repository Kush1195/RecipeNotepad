How to Make Hyderabadi Biryani : Clean the meat. Then in a pan add meat, salt, ginger garlic paste, red chilli powder, green chilli paste, sauteed brown onions, cardamom powder, cinnamon, cumin seeds, cloves, mace, mint leaves and lemon juice. Mix it well. Add curd, clarified butter, semi cooked rice, saffron, water and oil and mix it well.Now apply sticky dough on the sides of the pan. Cover with lid to seal it and cook for about 25 minutes. Hyderabadi Biryani is ready to eat. Garnish it with boiled eggs, sliced carrots, cucumbers and serve hot.
