How to Make Paneer Tikka : Marination for paneer tikka:Dry roast and pound cumin seeds, coriander seeds, brown cardamom, green cardamom, cloves, black pepper, star anise and shahee zeera in a mortar and pestle.In a bowl add ginger garlic paste, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, coriander powder, salt, kashmiri chilli powder, refined oil, coriander leaves chopped, mint leaves chopped, lime, dry mango powder, chaat masala, green chilli, whisked curd and hara masala. Mix them all. For grilling paneer tikka:In a tray spread some paneer cubes, julienne onion, capsicum and tomatoes.Mix the masala in the tray. Marinate the paneer cubes.Skewer the tikkas for roasting.Now keep the marinated paneer tikkas in the fridge for 45 minutes.Then grill the tikkas till cooked.Serve the paneer tikka hot.
