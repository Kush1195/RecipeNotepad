Tandori

Here is The Receipi of Tandoori. Add coriander powder and garam masala powder and mix well. Add 1/2 cup milk, 1/2 cup water and salt; mix and cook until oil comes on the surface or for approx. 4-5 minutes. Add paneer cubes, kasuri methi and cook for approx 2-minutes or until you get the desired consistency of gravy. Add fresh cream.
